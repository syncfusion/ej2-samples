Fleet Management Dashboard: A modern timeline scheduler with real-time analytics visualization. Manage driver schedules and monitor fleet metrics through an integrated analytics dashboard.

This sample demonstrates a fleet-management solution that pairs a Timeline Month Scheduler with a real-time Analytics Chart. The left panel shows driver routes grouped by resource; the right panel delivers analytics for quick insights and decision-making.

Key components

  • Timeline Scheduler: Monthly timeline with resource grouping for drivers and routes.
  • Analytics Dashboard: Three switchable views — Trip Count, Truck Capacity, and Longest Trips.
  • Real-time sync: Charts update immediately on event create/edit/delete.
  • Visual consistency: Chart bars inherit scheduler resource colors.
